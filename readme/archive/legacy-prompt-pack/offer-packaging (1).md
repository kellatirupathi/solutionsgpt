# Offer Packaging & Monetization — How to Price and Sell Each Solution

## PURPOSE
This file defines how to structure, price, and sell Solution Architect GPT's recommendations as real, sellable service packages.

---

## 💰 PRICING PHILOSOPHY

1. **Price by VALUE, not by hours** — Clients care about what they GAIN, not how long you worked
2. **Always offer 3 tiers** — Starter, Growth, Pro (this increases average deal size)
3. **Monthly retainer = sustainable business** — Always include MRR in every offer
4. **ROI framing** — Show the client how the solution PAYS FOR ITSELF

---

## 🤝 HOW TO PITCH TO A BUSINESS OWNER

**The 3-Question Close:**
1. "How much time does your staff spend manually following up for payments/appointments every week?"
2. "How many customers did you lose last month because no one followed up on time?"
3. "If we could automate that and bring those customers back — what would that be worth to you?"

Then present the solution. Then show ROI. Then close.

## 🧮 MARGIN CALCULATION RULES — HOW TO PRICE YOUR RETAINER

### The Formula
```
Retainer = Tool Cost + (Tool Cost × Margin %)
Minimum Margin = 40%
Target Margin  = 60–80%
```

### Margin Table by Business Size

| Client Size | Tool Cost/month | Min Retainer (40%) | Target Retainer (65%) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Solo / very small | ₹1,500–₹2,500 | ₹2,100–₹3,500 | ₹2,475–₹4,125 |
| Small (1–5 staff) | ₹2,000–₹4,000 | ₹2,800–₹5,600 | ₹3,300–₹6,600 |
| Medium (5–15 staff) | ₹4,000–₹8,000 | ₹5,600–₹11,200 | ₹6,600–₹13,200 |
| Growing (15–50 staff) | ₹8,000–₹15,000 | ₹11,200–₹21,000 | ₹13,200–₹24,750 |
| Large / multi-location | ₹15,000+ | ₹21,000+ | ₹24,750+ |

### Practical Pricing Rules
1. **Never quote below Tool Cost × 1.4** — that's your floor, not negotiable
2. **Quote in round numbers** — ₹5,000 not ₹4,947 (looks cleaner, easier to remember)
3. **Separate tool costs from your fee in the proposal** — builds trust, reduces objection
4. **Razorpay/payment gateway fees** — always on client's account, never in your retainer
5. **Meta WhatsApp charges** — client pays Meta directly via AiSensy dashboard
6. **Annual billing discount** — offer 10–15% off for annual prepay, improves your cash flow
7. **Setup fee = 4–6× one month's retainer** — this is the standard ratio

### Setup Fee Formula
```
Setup Fee = Monthly Retainer × 4  (minimum)
Setup Fee = Monthly Retainer × 6  (for complex or multi-module systems)

Example: Retainer = ₹6,000/month
Min Setup = ₹24,000 | Standard Setup = ₹36,000
```

### What Justifies Higher Setup Fees
- Custom UI or portal (Softr/Glide/React) → +₹8,000–₹15,000
- Razorpay/Stripe payment integration → +₹3,000–₹5,000
- Multi-location support → +₹5,000–₹10,000
- Staff training (per session) → +₹3,000
- Data migration from old system → +₹2,000–₹8,000
- Custom reports/dashboards → +₹3,000–₹6,000
