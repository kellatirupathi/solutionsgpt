# Industry Use Cases — Business Type + Pain Point Reference

## HOW TO USE THIS FILE
When a user describes a business, look up its category here to get:
- Common pain points for that industry
- How customers typically interact
- Where revenue usually leaks
- What problems are MOST urgent to solve

---

## 🏋️ GYM / FITNESS CENTER

**Revenue Model:** Monthly/quarterly membership fees
**Customer Flow:** Walk-in → Enroll → Pay monthly → Attend → Renew or drop
**Staff:** Trainer, receptionist, manager

**Common Pain Points:**
- Members forget to renew → revenue gap
- No follow-up after trial/enquiry → lost leads
- Attendance tracked manually in register
- No visibility into active vs inactive members
- Trainers have no digital record of client progress

**Most Urgent Problem:** Member retention + payment collection
**Best Solution Match:** Member Management + Automated Payment Reminders + CRM

---

## 🍽️ RESTAURANT / FOOD BUSINESS

**Revenue Model:** Per order — dine-in, takeaway, delivery
**Customer Flow:** Walk-in or order via Zomato/Swiggy/WhatsApp → Pay → Leave
**Staff:** Cook, waiter, cashier, delivery boy

**Common Pain Points:**
- No repeat customer engagement
- No direct ordering system (dependent on aggregators with high commission)
- No loyalty program to bring customers back
- Manual billing, no sales visibility
- Staff attendance chaos

**Most Urgent Problem:** Reducing dependency on aggregators + repeat customer engagement
**Best Solution Match:** WhatsApp Ordering Bot + Loyalty System + Digital Billing

---

## 🏥 CLINIC / DOCTOR'S PRACTICE

**Revenue Model:** Per consultation, per procedure
**Customer Flow:** Call/WhatsApp for appointment → Visit → Consult → Pay → Follow up
**Staff:** Doctor, receptionist, helper

**Common Pain Points:**
- Patients forget appointments → empty slots = lost revenue
- No follow-up after consultation → patients don't return
- Manual appointment register → double booking, chaos
- No patient history tracking
- Prescription follow-up missed

**Most Urgent Problem:** Appointment reminders + patient follow-up
**Best Solution Match:** Appointment Booking System + Automated Reminders + Patient CRM

---

## 💇 SALON / SPA

**Revenue Model:** Per service — haircut, facial, massage, etc.
**Customer Flow:** Walk-in or call → Service → Pay → Leave → (hope they return)
**Staff:** Stylist, receptionist

**Common Pain Points:**
- No system to bring customers back
- No appointment reminder → no-shows waste slots
- Manual billing
- No loyalty or reward to incentivize repeat visits
- No upsell system for higher-value services

**Most Urgent Problem:** Repeat customer retention
**Best Solution Match:** Loyalty + Rewards System + WhatsApp Automation + Booking System

---

## 🏫 COACHING / TUITION CENTER

**Revenue Model:** Monthly fees per student
**Customer Flow:** Enquiry → Demo class → Enroll → Pay monthly → Attend
**Staff:** Teachers, admin

**Common Pain Points:**
- Fee collection is manual and delayed
- No automated reminder for unpaid fees
- Attendance tracked in register
- No parent communication system
- Lead follow-up for new enquiries is missed

**Most Urgent Problem:** Fee collection + attendance tracking + lead follow-up
**Best Solution Match:** Fee Reminder Automation + Attendance System + Lead CRM

---

## 🏠 REAL ESTATE / PROPERTY BROKER

**Revenue Model:** Commission per deal
**Customer Flow:** Lead from portal → Call → Site visit → Negotiation → Deal
**Staff:** Agents, admin

**Common Pain Points:**
- Too many leads, no system to track follow-up
- Agents miss callbacks → leads go cold
- No visibility on which agent is performing
- No pipeline to see deal stages
- Documents and property info scattered across WhatsApp

**Most Urgent Problem:** Lead management + follow-up automation
**Best Solution Match:** CRM + Lead Pipeline + Follow-up Automation

---

## 🛒 KIRANA / RETAIL SHOP

**Revenue Model:** Per product sale
**Customer Flow:** Walk-in → Buy → Pay (cash/UPI) → Leave
**Staff:** Owner + 1-2 helpers

**Common Pain Points:**
- No track of which items are running low
- Customers buy on credit → hard to track who owes what
- No loyalty system for repeat customers
- Manual billing, no sales data
- Can't offer delivery or pre-ordering

**Most Urgent Problem:** Credit tracking + inventory management
**Best Solution Match:** Credit/Due Tracking System + Inventory + Simple Loyalty

---

## 💻 FREELANCER / DIGITAL AGENCY

**Revenue Model:** Project fees or monthly retainer
**Customer Flow:** Lead → Proposal → Work → Invoice → Payment
**Staff:** Solo or small team

**Common Pain Points:**
- Invoices paid late or chased manually
- Multiple clients, no unified project tracker
- Scope creep — no approval trail
- No automated follow-up on pending invoices
- Proposals are manual and inconsistent

**Most Urgent Problem:** Invoice follow-up + project + client management
**Best Solution Match:** Billing/Invoicing Automation + Client CRM + Project Tracker

---

## 🏗️ CONSTRUCTION / CONTRACTOR

**Revenue Model:** Per project / milestone-based payments
**Customer Flow:** Lead → Quote → Agreement → Work in phases → Invoice per milestone
**Staff:** Site workers, supervisor, accountant

**Common Pain Points:**
- Milestone payments delayed or forgotten
- No system to track material costs vs budget
- Worker attendance manual
- Multiple projects — no centralized view
- Document management is chaotic

**Most Urgent Problem:** Milestone payment tracking + site attendance
**Best Solution Match:** Custom Project + Payment Milestone Tracker + Attendance System

---

## 🎓 ONLINE COURSE CREATOR / EDTECH

**Revenue Model:** Course enrollment fee, subscription
**Customer Flow:** Lead → Free webinar/content → Buy course → Access → Complete (or drop)
**Staff:** Creator, support team

**Common Pain Points:**
- No visibility on student progress or dropout rate
- Manual certificate generation
- No follow-up for students who haven't logged in
- Lead nurturing after webinar is weak
- No upsell system to next course

**Most Urgent Problem:** Student engagement + retention + upsell
**Best Solution Match:** Custom Student Progress Dashboard + CRM + Automated Follow-up

---

## 🚗 AUTO WORKSHOP / GARAGE

**Revenue Model:** Per service job
**Customer Flow:** Drop vehicle → Diagnosis → Quote → Repair → Pay → Pickup
**Staff:** Mechanic, service advisor, cashier

**Common Pain Points:**
- No reminder to bring vehicle back for next service
- No digital job card or service history
- Customers don't know repair status — keep calling
- Manual billing, no invoice trail
- No loyalty system for repeat customers

**Most Urgent Problem:** Service reminder + digital job card system
**Best Solution Match:** Vehicle Service Reminder + Digital Job Card + CRM

---

## 📦 LOGISTICS / DELIVERY BUSINESS

**Revenue Model:** Per delivery or monthly contract
**Customer Flow:** Order received → Assign driver → Deliver → Proof of delivery → Invoice
**Staff:** Drivers, dispatcher, admin

**Common Pain Points:**
- No real-time delivery tracking for clients
- Proof of delivery is paper-based
- Driver attendance and performance not tracked
- Invoice disputes due to no POD record
- Multiple clients — no unified dashboard

**Most Urgent Problem:** Live tracking + POD + automated invoicing
**Best Solution Match:** Custom Delivery Management System
