## SAMPLE 1: GYM BUSINESS

**Input:** "I run a gym. Members are not renewing and payments are always late."

---

### 🏢 BUSINESS SNAPSHOT
This is a local gym operating on a monthly/quarterly membership model. Revenue is generated through membership fees. Customers interact through walk-in enrollment and WhatsApp. The business depends on member retention and timely fee collection for cash flow stability.

### 🔥 REAL PROBLEM IDENTIFIED
The gym is losing revenue from two directions simultaneously: members are churning silently (no one notices until it's too late) and payments are being collected manually with no enforcement — creating a cash flow gap every month.

### 📌 PAIN POINT BREAKDOWN
- **No expiry tracking** — Nobody knows which members expire this week/month until they stop showing up
- **Manual payment follow-up** — Staff awkwardly calls members to ask for fees — often too late, sometimes not at all
- **No re-engagement** — Inactive or lapsed members never receive a message to bring them back
- **No attendance visibility** — Owner has no data on who is attending regularly vs who has mentally already left
- **Missed renewal window** — By the time the owner notices a member hasn't renewed, the member has already joined elsewhere

### ✅ PRIMARY SOLUTION RECOMMENDED
**Solution Name:** Gym Member Management + Automated Payment Reminder System
**Solution Type:** Predefined Combo (Member/Subscription Management + Payment Reminder Automation)

A centralized member database connected to an automated WhatsApp communication system. Every member's plan, start date, expiry date, and payment status is tracked in real time. The system automatically sends WhatsApp reminders 7 days before expiry, on expiry day, and 3 days after — with a Razorpay payment link. If unpaid, an escalation message is sent to the owner. Inactive members (no attendance in 14 days) receive a re-engagement message automatically.

### 🎯 WHY THIS SOLUTION — NOT SOMETHING ELSE
1. **Revenue is directly tied to renewals** — solving expiry tracking and payment reminders directly impacts monthly revenue
2. **WhatsApp is the channel members already use** — sending reminders there is far more effective than email or calls
3. **Owner can't scale manual follow-up** — automation removes the dependency on staff memory
4. **Attendance data enables proactive retention** — knowing who hasn't come in 2 weeks lets you intervene before they leave

### 🌐 IS A WEBSITE NEEDED?
**Helpful but Secondary.** The core problem is retention and payments — not lead acquisition. A website may help attract new members eventually, but it won't fix churn. Focus on the retention system first.

### 🔄 ALTERNATIVE SOLUTIONS
1. **CRM Only (Zoho CRM)** — Tracks members but lacks automated payment reminders and WhatsApp integration out of the box. More complex to set up for this use case.
2. **Gym Management Software (Mindbody, Gymmaster)** — Full-featured but expensive (₹5,000–₹15,000/month) and overkill for a single-location gym. Less customizable.
3. **Manual Excel + Reminder Calls** — What they're doing now. Doesn't scale and relies on staff discipline.

### 🛠️ TECH STACK & BUILD APPROACH

| Component | Tool | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Member Database | Airtable | Visual, easy to manage, free tier works |
| Automation Engine | Make.com | Handles date-based triggers perfectly |
| WhatsApp Messaging | AiSensy or Interakt | Easy WhatsApp API, Indian market, affordable |
| Payment Collection | Razorpay | Auto-generates payment link in WhatsApp message |
| Owner Dashboard | Softr | No-code portal on top of Airtable |

**Build Difficulty:** Medium
**Estimated Build Time:** 7–10 days
**Approach:** No-Code / Low-Code

### 🔧 KEY FEATURES TO BUILD
- **Member Profile** — Name, phone, plan type, start date, expiry date, payment status
- **Auto Reminder Flow** — Day -7, Day 0, Day +3 WhatsApp messages with Razorpay link
- **Payment Status Update** — Auto-marks as Paid when Razorpay webhook fires
- **Attendance Log** — QR scan or manual check-in, linked to member profile
- **Inactive Member Alert** — Trigger if no check-in in 14 days → send re-engagement message
- **Owner Daily Summary** — WhatsApp message every morning showing: Expiring Today, Overdue, New Members
- **Renewal Report** — Weekly view of renewals received vs expected
- **Escalation Alert** — If member unpaid after day +3, alert owner directly

---

### 🔥 REAL PROBLEM IDENTIFIED
There is no structured client management system — every project lives in scattered WhatsApp threads, email chains, and personal notes. This leads to missed approvals, payment delays, and a poor client experience that hurts referrals.

### ✅ PRIMARY SOLUTION RECOMMENDED
**Solution Name:** Photographer Client Portal
**Solution Type:** ✳️ Custom Dynamic Solution (Not in predefined list)

A lightweight, per-client web portal where each client has a private login. The portal handles: mood board uploads and approvals, shot list sign-off, milestone payment tracking (advance + final), wedding day timeline, and final gallery delivery — all in one place. No more WhatsApp chaos.
